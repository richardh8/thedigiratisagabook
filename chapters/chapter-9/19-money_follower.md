## Money Follower

Is the financial conscience of transformation. They track where money is coming from, where it’s going, and how it creates value. They see everything through an efficiency and ROI lens, ensuring bold initiatives remain economically sustainable. For them, strategy is meaningless if it doesn’t make financial sense.

* Traits & Skills: Financial modeling, cost-benefit analysis, budget management, performance metrics.
* Organizational Fit: Vital in finance departments, CFO offices, investment committees, or strategic planning.
* Background: Commonly studied finance, accounting, economics, or business analytics. Many hold certifications like CFA, CPA, or FP&A.
* Goal: Align digital transformation with financial health, turning innovation into profitable, sustainable growth.
