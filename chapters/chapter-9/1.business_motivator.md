## Business Motivator

Is the restless engine that constantly asks: “Where can we grow? What value can we unlock?” This person has a commercial instinct, sharp market awareness, and an entrepreneurial eye for opportunities both inside and outside the organization. They are not satisfied with incremental improvements — they are opportunity-spotters who know how to translate ideas into tangible revenue streams.

* Traits & Skills: Strategic thinking, negotiation, financial acumen, opportunity mapping, and persuasive communication.
* Organizational Fit: Often found in strategy, business development, product management, or sales leadership.
* Background: Commonly with backgrounds in business administration, economics, marketing, or entrepreneurship, though many great Business Motivators come from engineering or even the creative arts, as long as they pair curiosity with commercial drive.
* Goal: Drive growth and ensure digital initiatives are not tech for tech’s sake, but engines of business impact.
