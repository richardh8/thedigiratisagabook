## Collaborative Agent

The Collaborative Agent is the facilitator of teamwork. They believe in collective intelligence and know that collaboration brings higher quality and creativity. 
They design, organize, and foster collaborative spaces, both physical and digital, to ensure productive teamwork.

* Traits & Skills: Facilitation, collaboration, group dynamics, conflict resolution, inclusivity.
* Organizational Fit: Found in Agile teams, cross-functional squads, HR, learning & development, and cultural transformation.
* Background: Often studied organizational psychology, collaboration methodologies (Agile, Scrum, Design Sprint), or education.
* Goal: Break silos, foster trust, and amplify innovation by making collaboration a natural organizational behavior.
