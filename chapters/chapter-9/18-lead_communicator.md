## Lead Communicator

Is the clarity enabler. They cut through noise and misunderstandings, ensuring that messages are assertive, fact-based, and aligned with shared goals. They unify people by building common ground and keeping dialogue constructive.
* Traits & Skills: Communication, facilitation, emotional intelligence, negotiation, clarity of expression.
* Organizational Fit: Strong presence in leadership, product management, internal communications, change management, and stakeholder-facing roles.
* Background: Typically from communications, business, leadership, or psychology, with experience in public speaking, negotiation, or facilitation.
* Goal: Eliminate friction caused by poor communication and ensure alignment across diverse teams and stakeholders.
