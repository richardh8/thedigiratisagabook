## Value Dreamer

Is the visionary who elevates the “why.” They are driven by purpose, not just profit, inspiring people to see transformation as part of a bigger story — a new market, a societal contribution, or a leap in human experience. They motivate by showing what could be, enchanting others with values that attract talent, partners, and customers alike.

* Traits & Skills: Vision crafting, purpose-driven leadership, narrative building, inspirational communication.
* Organizational Fit: Often sits in executive leadership, strategy, innovation hubs, or CSR functions.
* Background: Many studied philosophy, leadership, business strategy, or social sciences, though some emerge from founders’ grit and lived experience.
* Goal: Keep transformation grounded in meaning, inspiring loyalty and sustainable innovation.
