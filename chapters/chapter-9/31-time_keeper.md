## Time Keeper

In the age of agility, the Time Keeper ensures flow. They are masters of rhythm, pushing teams to deliver at the right tempo — not too slow to lose the market, not too fast to burn out. They see bottlenecks before they appear, orchestrate cadence across departments, and keep transformation efforts lean and timely.

* Traits & Skills: Project and program management, Agile/Scrum mastery, process optimization, metrics orientation.
* Organizational Fit: Essential in PMOs, Agile coaching roles, engineering leadership, and operations teams.
* Background: Frequently with education in project management, systems engineering, computer science, or business operations.
* Goal: Keep the organization moving at a sustainable yet competitive pace, ensuring innovation is delivered “on time and in time.”
