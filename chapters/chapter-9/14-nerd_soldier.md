## Nerd Soldier

Is a hands-on technologist. They understand how things actually work, keep up with evolving technologies, and experiment with practical applications for the organization. They bridge the gap between hype and reality, ensuring that innovation is grounded in feasibility.
* Traits & Skills: Technical expertise, experimentation, problem-solving, pragmatism, resilience.
* Organizational Fit: Found in IT, engineering, DevOps, cybersecurity, R&D, and anywhere that technology implementation matters.
* Background: Strong technical foundations in computer science, engineering, or IT. Many are practitioners with hands-on certifications (cloud, security, data, etc.).
