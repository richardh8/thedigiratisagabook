## Risk Ravenous

Individuals are fearless in facing uncertainty. They know risk cannot be eliminated, only understood, mitigated, and — when well managed — turned into an advantage. They are skilled in reading markets, regulations, cybersecurity threats, and economic conditions, acting as guardians of resilience. For them, the biggest risk is doing nothing.
* Traits & Skills: Risk modeling, compliance, scenario planning, cybersecurity, regulatory knowledge.
* Organizational Fit: Core in risk management, compliance, cybersecurity, finance, and governance functions.
* Background: Come from law, economics, cybersecurity, actuarial sciences, or audit backgrounds.
* Goal: Help the organization move boldly while staying safe, ensuring growth and innovation are not derailed by blind spots.
