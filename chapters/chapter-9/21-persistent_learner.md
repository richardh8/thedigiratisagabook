## Persistent Learner

Is a lifelong learner. They see every challenge as an opportunity to learn and improve. They continuously acquire new knowledge and apply it daily, embedding adaptability and curiosity into the organization.

* Traits & Skills: Curiosity, humility, adaptability, continuous learning, resilience.
* Organizational Fit: Valuable everywhere, but especially in emerging technology, innovation, and evolving roles where upskilling is constant.
* Background: Less about formal education, more about attitude. Often supported by self-learning, online education, certifications, and cross-functional experiences.
* Goal: Keep the organization fresh and adaptable by never letting knowledge stagnate.
