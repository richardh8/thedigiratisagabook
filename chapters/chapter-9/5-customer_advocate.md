## Customer Advocate

Is the voice of the customer inside the organization. They deeply understand user needs, represent them in decisions, and ensure that products, services, and processes always prioritize customer outcomes.

* Traits & Skills: Empathy, customer research, advocacy, service design, negotiation.
* Organizational Fit: Found in CX, product management, customer success, marketing, and service design.
* Background: Often from marketing, design, customer support, or business strategy, with expertise in customer experience (CX), design thinking, or ethnographic research.
* Goal: Align business success with customer satisfaction — making sure customers’ needs are never sidelined
