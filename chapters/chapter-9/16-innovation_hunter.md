## Innovation Hunter

Is the scout of the future. Constantly scanning both inside and outside the organization, they identify trends, technologies, and practices that could become the next growth engine. They know how to separate hype from true opportunity, contextualize innovation, and bring it to life.
* Traits & Skills: Market foresight, trend scouting, prototyping, ecosystem mapping, open innovation.
* Organizational Fit: Found in R&D, innovation labs, corporate strategy, and digital transformation teams.
* Background: Diverse, but often design, engineering, entrepreneurship, or technology management. Many are serial innovators with experience across industries.
* Goal: Spot, shape, and scale innovations before competitors, keeping the organization on the leading edge.
