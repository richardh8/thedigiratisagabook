## Storyteller

Is the voice of meaning. They craft and share narratives that bring strategy, transformation, and vision to life. Through analogies, metaphors, and stories, they connect data with emotion, making messages memorable and inspiring action. They translate complexity into human experience.

* Traits & Skills: Communication, narrative design, empathy, persuasion, multimedia fluency.
* Organizational Fit: Impactful in marketing, leadership, HR, training, and customer experience.
* Background: Often from communications, journalism, literature, film, or marketing, with strong exposure to business strategy and behavioral psychology.
* Goal: Inspire people, align them around vision, and make abstract transformation journeys tangible and relatable.
