## Ecosystem Connector

Sees the organization not as a silo but as a node in a larger web of partners, suppliers, regulators, startups, and customers. They know what is core, what can be outsourced, and where partnerships can multiply value. They are natural orchestrators, weaving ecosystems where collaboration becomes a competitive advantage.

* Traits & Skills: Systems thinking, networking, strategic partnerships, negotiation, ecosystem mapping.
* Organizational Fit: Thrive in alliances, procurement, corporate strategy, venture building, and partner management.
* Background: Strong roots in business strategy, law, economics, or international relations, though many technologists evolve here by learning to connect dots outside IT.
