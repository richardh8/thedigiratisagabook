## Change Adapter

Is the resilient navigator of uncertainty. In a world where disruption is constant, they anticipate shifts, prepare the organization, and guide others through transitions with empathy and clarity. They neutralize resistance and ensure that people feel included and supported rather than threatened.

* Traits & Skills: Flexibility, emotional intelligence, communication, foresight, stakeholder management.
* Organizational Fit: Essential in change management, HR, transformation offices, and program management.
* Background: Often studied organizational psychology, human resources, communications, or leadership development. Certifications in change management (e.g., Prosci, Kotter) are common.
* Goal: Ensure transformations succeed by making people ready, willing, and able to adopt new behaviors, tools, and ways of working.
