## Subject Curious

This individual is a perpetual learner. They are explorers who scan the horizon for emerging topics, technologies, and methodologies. Curiosity drives them to test, prototype, and contextualize new concepts, often before the rest of the organization notices. They bring the “what if” mindset that fuels discovery and innovation.

* Traits & Skills: Curiosity, experimentation, trend spotting, fast learning, research synthesis.
* Organizational Fit: Found across innovation labs, R&D teams, foresight functions, or embedded in product squads as early adopters.
* Background: These professionals come from diverse disciplines — science, humanities, design, or tech — what unites them is their hunger to keep learning. Many also pursue certifications, online courses, meet ups, and side projects.
* Goal: Ensure the organization never misses the next wave of opportunity by constantly bringing insights into play.
