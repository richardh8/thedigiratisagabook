## Lab Experiencer

Is the innovation incubator. They believe ideas must be tested, evolved, and validated before scaling. They manage budgets like investors, oversee incubations, evaluate outcomes, and communicate progress. They thrive in controlled experimentation to reduce risks while unlocking opportunities.
* Traits & Skills: Experimentation mindset, portfolio management, analytical evaluation, risk management.
* Organizational Fit: Natural fit in innovation labs, R&D departments, venture arms, or strategy teams.
* Background: Often trained in innovation management, entrepreneurship, design thinking, or finance. Many come from product management or corporate venturing backgrounds.
* Goal: Build a systematic way for the organization to test ideas, generate learning, and scale what works.
