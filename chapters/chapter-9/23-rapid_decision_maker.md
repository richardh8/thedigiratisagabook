## Rapid Decision Maker

Thrives where speed is survival. They gather relevant data, consult experts, and cut through ambiguity to act decisively. They know the cost of delay can be higher than the cost of a mistake. Using analytics, intuition, and strong networks, they make choices that keep the organization moving forward.

* Traits & Skills: Analytical thinking, decisiveness, stakeholder alignment, data literacy, scenario planning.
* Organizational Fit: Crucial in executive leadership, crisis management, operations, product delivery, and trading/financial roles.
* Background: Frequently trained in business administration, finance, or data analytics; often seasoned by high-pressure roles that sharpen decision-making under uncertainty.
