## Quality Balancer

Are guardians of excellence who know perfection is not always the goal — value delivery is. They have the wisdom to balance speed with rigor, risk with ambition, and efficiency with continuous improvement. They prevent organizations from collapsing under the weight of endless rework, bureaucracy, or uncontrolled experimentation.
* Traits & Skills: Process design, risk management, Six Sigma/Lean, quality assurance, and pragmatic decision-making.
* Organizational Fit: Commonly in operations, QA teams, compliance, DevOps, or program management.
* Background: Often studied industrial engineering, operations management, quality engineering, or computer science.
* Goal: Ensure transformation creates sustainable, reliable value — keeping customers and regulators confident without stifling progress.
