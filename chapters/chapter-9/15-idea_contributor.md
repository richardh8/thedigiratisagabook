## Idea Contributor

Is the courageous spark in every team. They generate ideas without fear, share them openly, and quickly seek validation. They don’t cling to ownership but invite others to refine and co-create. They know that innovation thrives when ideas are socialized, tested, and iterated.

* Traits & Skills: Creativity, courage, brainstorming, experimentation, collaboration.
* Organizational Fit: Everywhere — but particularly in innovation labs, product teams, and cross-functional workshops.
* Background: From any discipline; what matters is openness, creative confidence, and willingness to experiment. Many train in design thinking or brainstorming methods.
* Goal: Keep the pipeline of possibilities alive by ensuring that ideas are continuously generated, tested, and evolved into impactful solutions.
