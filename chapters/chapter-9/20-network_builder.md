## Network Builder

Is the connector of opportunities. They cultivate relationships across people, companies, and industries, always envisioning how collaborations can lead to win-win results. They are skilled at seeing synergies where others see boundaries, and mobilize networks to accelerate projects and transformation initiatives.

* Traits & Skills: Relationship building, trust creation, negotiation, social intelligence, strategic collaboration.
* Organizational Fit: Critical in business development, partnerships, ecosystems, and leadership roles that require cross-company collaboration.
* Background: Frequently from business, international relations, communications, or sales/partnerships backgrounds. They combine networking skills with strategic thinking.
* Goal: Expand the organization’s reach, build alliances, and create opportunities that no single entity could achieve alone — ensuring access to new markets, ideas, and resources.
